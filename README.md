## 📋 About This Project

This is a free, open-source **NY DMV Practice Test** tool designed to help New York residents pass their DMV written knowledge test on the first attempt.

Whether you're getting your first driver's license, renewing after a lapse, or obtaining a Real ID — this practice test covers everything you need.

🔗 **Live Version:** [https://dmvappointmentny.us/ny-dmv-practice-test/](https://dmvappointmentny.us/ny-dmv-practice-test/)

---

## ✅ Features

- **30 full-length practice tests** — Each with 20 questions
- **600 total questions** covering all NY DMV topics
- Instant feedback — see correct/incorrect answers immediately
- Score tracker — track your progress across tests
- Mobile-friendly — works on all devices
- No login required — 100% free

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

- Traffic signs and signals
- Right-of-way rules
- Speed limits and safe following distance
- Alcohol, drugs, and driving laws
- Parking rules in New York
- Highway and expressway driving
- Pedestrian and bicycle laws
- NY-specific DMV regulations

- First-time drivers applying for a NY learner's permit
